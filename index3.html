5 Best Travel Tips for you

1. Wake Up Early

Wake up early to have more time to travel around.
It’s a good time for photos due to soft diffused light, and usually easier to interact with local people.

2. Take Lots of Photos

Lots of photos don’t cost anything, they’re easy to share with others, and they don’t take up space in your luggage.
You may only see these places and meet these people once in your lifetime.
Remember them forever with photos.

3. Meet Local People

Meet and learn from those who live in the country you’re visiting.
People enrich your travels more than sights do.

4. Eat Local Food

Taste a bit of everything when you travel, especially if you don’t know what it is.
Ask local people for recommendations.
Eat street food from vendors with big lines out front.
